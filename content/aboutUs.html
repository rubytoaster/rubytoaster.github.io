The Art of the Possible (AoP) is a constraints-based management system designed to create a workforce culture focused on efficient process execution. AoP creates a culture that is focused on identifying and urgently eliminating process constraints affecting the process critical path during execution. This culture relies on the skills, abilities and forward thinking of the enterprise workforce to create the necessary teamwork to enable speed.
The AoP App
The intent of the AoP App is to communicate the core tenants and guiding principles of AoP to ensure these ideals become the foundation for daily operations. It also provides examples, activities and training on how to get started and understand the core principles of AoP. Ultimately, the AoP App is about utilizing a methodical approach to improving processes in order to obtain AoP results.
