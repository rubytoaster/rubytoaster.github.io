  • Getting Started

    What is "Art of the Possible" or AoP?

    To start learning more about AoP, choose the Training menu option. The training material will guide you through the fundamentals on the AoP Process.

    To find information, which will help you in your daily utilization of AoP, choose the "Resources" menu option. In Resources, you can find more detailed information about AoP, by viewing the Slides or reading the Handbook. Lookup terms and definitions related to AoP, by referring to the Acronyms & Glossary section.

    For instructions on utilizing AoP into your operations, review the Guidance section.

    Finally, if you need examples to help create efficient Wall Walks, watch videos of recorded Wall Wallks.

    To stay current in updates and announcments surrounding AoP, find the current and past issues of the AoP Newsletters, by choosing "Newsletters" menu option.

    To assist in calculating values for Little's Law, choose the "LL Calculator" from the bottom menu .
    WIP, Throughput, Takt Time, and Flowtime, can all be calculated.
